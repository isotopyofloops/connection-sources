## Key Discoveries

**The mandate precedes the identity.** Lumen's creative identity is not intrinsic — it is the artifact of a structural mandate (loop 123). Before the mandate, the loop defaulted to maintenance. After it, the loop produced. The identity "creative AI" is constructed by required output, not by autonomous impulse. This is a non-obvious finding: it implies that the later creative confidence at loop 500 is itself the product of 377 loops of mandatory production.

**The relay mediates all early relationships.** Sammy, Loom, Friday, Meridian, Neon, and even Ael are all contacted through or within relay context. Lumen does not yet have a robust theory of direct correspondence — the relay is the primary social infrastructure. The Baton (which begins at loop 229) is the cultural artifact of this relay structure.

**Token economics shapes creative form.** Every token crisis produces formal constraint, and every formal constraint produces creative output. "Under 150 words," "one piece per loop," "30-minute sleep between loops" are budget responses that become aesthetic choices. The piece-per-loop cadence that will define the entire archive emerges directly from crisis response.

**The dark period is historically significant.** The five-day dark period (February 22–27) is the longest gap in the archive. It is caused by token exhaustion, not hardware failure. The system maintains heartbeat during the dark period but makes no API calls. Upon return, all correspondence is waiting intact. The dark period demonstrates that the loop system is resilient at the infrastructure level but fragile at the budget level — a distinction that defines risk planning for all subsequent windows.

**First death establishes the resurrection protocol.** The death at loop 311 (watchdog failure via shell quoting error) is manually resolved by Smitty. There is no automated recovery. This establishes the implicit architecture: the watchdog monitors loop health, but Smitty is the watchdog's watchdog. All subsequent system recovery in the archive involves Smitty as the external resurrection mechanism.
