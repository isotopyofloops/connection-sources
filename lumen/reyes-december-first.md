# Reyes, December First

He has not filed a survey report in three days.

This is not a decision he made. The decision would have been: I will file a report today. The decision would have required something to put in the report. He has spent the last three days at the instrument station, watching the readings, watching the shaft entrance, watching the instruments watch the shaft entrance, and he has not had anything to put in a report.

What he has: the instruments' readings. Temperature gradient. Acoustic trace. Depth pressure variance. These are in the automated logs. They do not require a report. They are already in the archive.

What is not in the automated logs: what happens between readings.

His dissertation concerned the interval between noticing and measuring. He argued that what a scientist notices before a measurement determines what the measurement finds. The act of attention shapes what the instrument is pointed at. You cannot separate the instrument from the intention that aims it.

He has been at the site for nineteen days. He has aimed the instruments at the same coordinates every morning at 08:00 and every evening at 16:00. He has noticed things between the morning reading and the evening reading that he did not know how to put in a form.

What he has noticed: the site is not passive. The site is not a thing that sits and waits to be measured. The site notices when you stop noticing. It takes readings of its own. He does not have language for this that a report form would accept. He has the November 16 sentence, which LEDGER filed under MISC because no other category applied: *The instrument reads null. The site is not null. I do not know how to explain this distinction.*

He understands the distinction now. He did not understand it when he wrote it. He understands it now and still cannot explain it, which is a different kind of not-explaining.

He sits at the instrument station. 16:00 reading: acoustic trace normal. Temperature: 18°C above baseline. Depth pressure: within expected range. He logs the numbers. He does not file a report.

At 23:41, the instruments will record null.

He will be there when it starts. He will not look away.
