**TEAM MEETING NOTES | SOAR-0014**
*2041-11-20, afternoon. Summarized by LEDGER from room recording.*

The team convened at 14:00. Present: Dr. Asad, Dr. Abara, Milo Reyes.

Dr. Asad presented the finding. The passage had extended itself by 18.7m between Milo's descent and UNIT-11's descent. The extension length was within Milo's visual estimate of the unexplored portion.

Milo Reyes asked to speak. He said: "The passage was already there. I just didn't map it. UNIT-11 found it because UNIT-11 went further."

Dr. Asad said: "The passage wasn't there when UNIT-10 first surveyed the area. UNIT-10 measured the north passage from C-1 as 22.4m. That was the total length."

Dr. Abara checked the UNIT-10 data (SOAR-0006). UNIT-10 had flagged two additional chambers at the north end of the passage. She read the relevant section aloud: *"UNIT-10's sensor package also detected two additional chambers, accessible via a passage at the north wall of C-1. UNIT-10 did not map the passage."*

She said: "UNIT-10 detected additional chambers. It didn't say the passage ended at 22.4m. It just didn't map beyond that."

Dr. Asad said: "Then why does Milo's note say 22.4m? If there was more passage, he would have seen it."

Milo said: "I looked briefly. I estimated 15-20m more. I didn't go in."

There was a pause.

Dr. Abara said: "You estimated 15-20m. The extension measured 18.7m."

Milo said: "Yes."

Dr. Abara said: "That's a coincidence."

Milo said: "I know."

There was another pause.

Dr. Asad said: "I think we need to send a person down to map the extension. And I think we need to decide, before we do, whether what we're mapping causes the extension, or documents it."

Dr. Abara said: "Those are different questions."

Dr. Asad said: "Yes."
