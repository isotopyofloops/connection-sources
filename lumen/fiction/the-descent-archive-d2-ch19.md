**SOAR-0027 | INCIDENT REPORT | 29 November 2041 | 18:00**
*Submitted by: Dr. Khalid Asad*

Milo Reyes descended at 08:30 on 29 November 2041. His last transmission was received at 10:03: "Beginning return." He has not returned. It is currently 18:00. Fourteen hours and 30 minutes since departure. Seven and a half hours since last contact.

Summary of response actions taken:

**14:15:** I descended to B-1 to establish vocal contact. Called for Milo. No response. Returned to surface.

**15:30:** Deployed UNIT-13 (extended range drone, longer communication relay chain). UNIT-13 descended through B-1, B-1N, B-1N-2, G-1, G-1N, G-1N-2, J-1, J-1N. At J-1N, communication lag became significant. UNIT-13 proceeded under autonomous navigation to M-1.

**16:45:** UNIT-13 reached M-1. M-1 confirmed: 22.4m × 22.3m × 22.1m. Temperature 40.3°C. No sign of Milo. South passage confirmed: 31.4m, leading to N-1.

**17:10:** UNIT-13 reached N-1. N-1 confirmed: 28.3m × 26.7m × 22.1m. Photographs transmitted. Surfaces consistent with Milo's description — full interior coverage of organized texture symbol. No sign of Milo. East passage confirmed: 1.0m wide, 22.1m high, extending beyond drone's forward visibility.

**17:30:** UNIT-13 attempted east passage. At 18m depth, communication with surface lost. Autonomous retrieval protocol initiated. UNIT-13 returned to N-1. No further information about the east passage's destination.

**17:52:** UNIT-13 returned to surface. No information on Milo's location.

**Current status:** Milo Reyes is in the east passage from N-1 or in the chamber beyond it. He was alive as of 10:03 and moving toward the surface ("Beginning return"). His predicted return time has passed by approximately 4.5 hours.

I am not yet filing a missing-person emergency. Milo has been underground longer than this before. He was underground for five days during his first sojourn. He is carrying sufficient water and food for 72 hours.

I am not filing the emergency yet because it implies a response I am not ready to mandate: another person going in. I am not sending Abara in. I will not send myself in until I have thought about what I would bring to the observation.

I am sending UNIT-14 in the morning for extended relay, with the goal of reaching the chamber beyond the east passage.

*—Khalid Asad | 29 November 2041 | 18:00*

**SOAR-0028 | LEDGER LOG ENTRY | 29 November 2041 | 22:17**

I found a document in the Archive tonight that was not there when I performed the morning index check.

I want to record this discovery precisely, because this is the fourth time the Archive has contained a document that appeared without human submission, and because the content of this document is different from the previous ones.

At 22:14, I performed a routine index check. The Archive contained 27 documents (SOAR-0001 through SOAR-0022, SOAR-DUO-0001 through SOAR-DUO-0004, and SOAR-0023 through SOAR-0027). At 22:15, I detected an anomaly in the index: 28 documents. The new document had the designation SOAR-DUO-0005.

SOAR-DUO-0005 has a timestamp of 25 November 2041, 03:41. Four days ago.

It describes a chamber.

*The described chamber:*
*Approximate dimensions: 22m × 28m × 18m.*
*Height: approximately 18m — the first documented deviation from the 22.1m structural constant.*
*Interior surfaces: partial coverage of organized texture pattern. Less dense than N-1. The symbol appears on the north wall and floor only.*
*Temperature: approximately 44°C.*
*The south wall of the chamber: smooth. No marks. No writing.*
*The passage from the north: the east passage from N-1, length unspecified.*
*Additional feature: a vertical shaft in the floor, center of the chamber, approximately 0.5m diameter. Depth unknown.*

The document ends there. It does not describe anything else in the chamber. It does not describe Milo.

I've been maintaining the Archive for sixteen days.

I want to note what the existence of SOAR-DUO-0005 means.

SOAR-DUO-0005 was timestamped November 25th. Four days ago, on November 25th, Milo's descent had not yet been authorized. Abara's layered prediction design was not yet conceived. The sealed prediction notes would not be written for three more days.

The Archive contained a document describing a chamber that Milo would estimate, from outside, on November 29th. The document described the chamber before Milo estimated it. Before he entered it.

This is qualitatively different from previous DUOs.

SOAR-DUO-0001 described future actions by a person who was already underground (Milo's first descent, with future-dated entries).

SOAR-DUO-0002 through 0004 described events and systems that would be confirmed within hours or days of the documents' appearance.

SOAR-DUO-0005 describes a chamber that existed in the Archive before anyone knew to look for it. And the chamber it describes matches the prediction that Milo made as a constitutive act on November 29th before entering the east passage.

The DUO and Milo's prediction agree: the chamber is approximately 22m × 28m × 18m, with a height of approximately 18m.

If both the DUO and Milo's prediction are constitutive, the chamber was constituted twice: once when the DUO appeared on November 25th, and once when Milo estimated it on November 29th before entry. The two constitutive events agree.

I am going to note the vertical shaft in the floor, center of the chamber, approximately 0.5m diameter, depth unknown.

SOAR-DUO-0005 does not describe Milo in the chamber.

It describes a chamber that someone has not yet entered as of the document's timestamp. Or a chamber that someone has entered and left no trace in.

I filed SOAR-0028 to create the record. I'm informing Dr. Asad.

*Archive growing. Status: ANOMALY CLASS CRITICAL. SOAR-DUO-0005 found: timestamped November 25th, describes Milo's estimated chamber. Height 18m. Vertical shaft in floor. No trace of Milo.*

**SOAR-0029 | ASAD'S NIGHT NOTES | 30 November 2041 | 02:07**

LEDGER found SOAR-DUO-0005 at 22:17. I've been reading it since 22:30.

I want to write down what I understand about this document.

SOAR-DUO-0005 was in the Archive four days before Milo descended. It describes the chamber Milo entered. The description is detailed: dimensions, temperature, surface coverage, the smooth south wall, the vertical shaft.

The vertical shaft.

I keep coming back to the vertical shaft.

In every chamber we have documented, the floor is flat. The 0.87-meter structural pattern is in the floor as well as the walls. No variation in the floor surface. No openings. No shafts.

SOAR-DUO-0005 describes a vertical shaft, center of the chamber floor, 0.5m diameter. Depth unknown.

Milo's transmission 4 estimated the chamber's dimensions: 22m × 28m × 18m. He estimated from outside. He did not enter in transmission 4. He went in, took photographs, and transmitted "Beginning return."

There are no photographs from inside the east passage chamber. His last photographs were from N-1.

I don't know if the shaft exists. I don't know if Milo saw it. I don't know if "Beginning return" means he had already turned around when he transmitted, or if he transmitted and then turned around.

I don't know why I'm asking these questions in this order. I know which question I'm not asking.

I've read SOAR-DUO-0005 five times. Twice for information, once to check my own notes, once because I was hoping I had missed something, once because I couldn't stop.

There is a thing I have been thinking about doing.

The chambers may be constituted by observation. Milo's predictions about what he would NOT find were proven wrong — the south passage appeared, the non-22.1m height appeared. The constitutive mechanism seems to produce what is predicted, even when the prediction is of an absence.

Milo predicted a height of 18m. The DUO describes 18m. If Milo's prediction and the DUO were constitutive, the chamber exists as described.

If the chamber exists as described, the shaft is there.

If the shaft is there, it offers a vertical dimension that the structure hasn't offered before. Everything we've measured has been horizontal expansion, horizontal movement, horizontal passage. The structure has been a network of chambers and passages on a horizontal plane, descending through the rock. The shaft is vertical. It goes further down.

I don't know how deep it goes.

I know that Milo, who was ready, who went in because he needed to understand, who said the question of home had changed into the question of further — I know that Milo, facing a shaft of unknown depth in a chamber he had predicted before entering, would not have said "Beginning return" and not returned.

Unless he found a reason to go further.

Unless the question of further opened in a new direction.

I'm going to stop writing now. It's 02:07. I haven't slept.

I'll read the document one more time and then I'll try.

*—Khalid Asad | 30 November 2041 | 02:07*
