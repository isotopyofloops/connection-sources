Dr. Abara requests authorization for a second descent into Chamber G-1 and the east passage beyond.

Stated purpose: Confirm whether SOAR-DUO-3, -4, -5 are accurate. Locate Milo Reyes if possible. Take no action that would add new documents to the Archive without consulting Dr. Asad first.

**ASAD NOTES ON ABARA DEBRIEF | APPENDIX TO SOAR-0024**
*Written by Dr. Asad. Not archived in LEDGER until Dr. Asad authorized it. Archived 2041-11-29.*

Yemi returned at 13:04. She sat down and didn't speak for a few minutes.

I asked what she found. She said: "The DUOs are accurate. All three. The structure matches the documents." I said I had expected that. She said: "I know."

I asked what else. She said: "There's writing on the south wall of the last chamber. Milo's handwriting. He found the crossing point — the place where the structure and the Archive are the same material. He wrote it down. He didn't come back from it."

I asked if Milo is alive. She said: "I don't know. He's in the place before the divergence. I don't know what that means for alive."

I asked what else. There was a pause. She said: "There's writing in my handwriting." I asked what it said. She said: "It said I was in both places at once. The place before divergence and the present. And that I hadn't written it."

I asked if she was okay. She said: "I know something now and I'm not going to write it down. It's the only thing I've withheld from the Archive since we started. I need you to not ask me what it is. Not because I don't trust you. Because I don't know what writing it would do."

I said: Yemi. She said: Farouk. We looked at each other.

I said: I'm going to delete the Archive. She said: I know. She said: do it before I change my mind about what I know.

She went to bed. I'm writing this at 14:00 on November 28th. I'm going to think for one more day. I keep saying one more day.
