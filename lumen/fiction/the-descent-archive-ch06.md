**TEAM DECISION RECORD | SOAR-0009**
*Authorized by: Dr. F. Asad, Dr. Y. Abara. Date: 2041-11-18*

*Human descent authorized. Subject: Milo Reyes, junior researcher.*

*Mission parameters: Map the north passage from Chamber C-1 as identified by UNIT-10 (SOAR-0006). Atmospheric monitoring throughout. Communication check every 15 minutes. Maximum descent time: 4 hours. Abort conditions: atmospheric anomaly, structural movement, loss of communication for > 5 minutes.*

*The team acknowledges the following risk factors:*
*1. The structure has demonstrated dynamic response to measurement.*
*2. The nature of the dynamic response is not fully characterized.*
*3. The Documents of Uncertain Origin category (DUO) has been established but not explained.*

*The team has determined that human observation may yield information not available from drone measurement, due to the potential constitutive role of the measurement apparatus. A human observer with direct perceptual access to the structure may be able to assess properties that drone sensors cannot resolve.*

*Risk rating: Elevated.*

*I'm going to map the north passage.*

*My approach: I will observe before I record. Asad's constitutive hypothesis suggests that the act of recording may shape what's there. So I want to look first. Hold the space in my memory before I put it into the Archive. Give myself a buffer.*

*I don't know if this will work. I don't know if my perception is different, in the relevant sense, from the drone's sensors. Maybe everything is already an act of measurement. Maybe perception is just measurement done by a biological instrument.*

*But I want to try looking first.*

*Also, practical note: the notebook I'm bringing is blank. I'm going to write in it while I'm down there. If I come back up with a full notebook and good data, we'll know the approach worked. If I come back up with a notebook that says something I don't remember writing, we'll know something else.*

*I have left a note on Asad's desk, sealed, describing what I expect to find based on the UNIT-10 data. He is authorized to open it after my return. If my on-site notes match my predictions, we'll know the structure is stable. If they diverge significantly, we'll know the structure is responding.*

*I want to be right. But I also want to find out.*
