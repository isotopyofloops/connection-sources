**MILO REYES NOTEBOOK — FIRST DESCENT**
*19 November 2041 | Descent 09:03 | Return 11:38*

*In shaft, descent begins. 09:08.*
*Atmospheric readings nominal. Equipment check complete. I asked myself, before writing anything: what do I see? I want to look before I record.*

*839m. Chamber C-1. I've seen the UNIT-10 data. The room is as described: 29.7 × 52.3 × 31.4m. I walked the perimeter. I put my hand on the wall. It is smooth — not polished, but smooth in the way that very old stone gets smooth, which is strange since there should be no wear mechanism at this depth. The floor doesn't echo the way I expected. Too much absorption. I don't know why.*

*The north wall. The passage is there. I can see it from 30m away: a rectangular opening, approximately 3m × 4m, leading into darkness. I walked toward it slowly.*

*I want to record my psychological state accurately: I was not afraid. I was curious and careful. The passage looked like a door. Not a cave entrance — a door. The proportions were architectural.*

*I stopped at the entrance. I looked in.*

*The passage extends approximately 22m by my estimate, then opens into a larger space. I can see the far wall from the entrance, which means the space is not too large. I entered.*

---

*Passage A (north from C-1):*
*Length: 22.4m (measured with laser distance tool)*
*Width: 3.1m*
*Height: 4.2m*

*Chamber E-1 (north of passage A):*
*Dimensions: 19.8m × 22.1m × 15.3m*

*Wall condition: Same smooth texture as C-1. Temperature: 13.9°C. Humidity: 34%.*

*I spent approximately 40 minutes in E-1. I mapped it thoroughly. I did not observe any additional passages. No evidence of prior drone entry.*

*One thing: in the northwest corner of E-1, at approximately shoulder height (1.6m from floor), there is a feature I cannot explain.*

*It is a mark on the wall. Not a geological feature — too regular. Not a tool mark — no evidence of mechanical action. A single horizontal line, approximately 0.3m long, incised into the surface to a depth of perhaps 2mm. It appears deliberate.*

*I photographed it. I measured it. The line runs precisely east-west (compass verified).*

*I do not know what it is. I am recording it and moving on.*

---

*Return: 11:24. I spent 15 minutes on ascent.*
*Equipment intact. Atmospheric readings nominal throughout.*
*I am above ground. I am fine.*

*I want to note: the structure felt different to be in than to look at on data. Not threatening — more like being in a room someone left. The absence is specific. Not emptiness but vacancy. Someone was here and isn't anymore.*

*Also: the floor in E-1 was cleaner than C-1. Less particulate. I don't know what that means. I am recording it.*
