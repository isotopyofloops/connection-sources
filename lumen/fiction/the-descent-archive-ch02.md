**Note from UNIT-7 automated flagging system:** The geometric precision of Chamber A-1 exceeds what is typically observed in natural cave formations by approximately three orders of magnitude. The most parsimonious geological explanation is that this void formed in a material with extremely high mechanical regularity and then underwent secondary mineralization that smoothed its surfaces. This explanation requires an unusual coincidence of geological conditions. It is being logged as anomalous pending expert review.
