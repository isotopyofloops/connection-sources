## CHAPTER TWENTY-EIGHT: APPENDIX — DOCUMENTS OF UNCERTAIN ORIGIN

*Not submitted through LEDGER. Compiled by the editor, February–March 2042.*
*Editor's note: 57 Documents of Uncertain Origin were found in or associated with the LEDGER archive. Nine appeared before the acceleration (November 14th through December 1st). Forty-seven appeared during the acceleration (December 2nd through December 5th). One appeared after the deletion.*
*A complete list follows. Documents marked [KEY] are reproduced in part or in full elsewhere in this archive.*

---

**SOAR-DUO-0001**
*Appeared: 14 November 2041 | Source field: "The Archive"*
Described the arrival of the survey team on November 13th. Accurate. Filed one day after the arrival it described.

**SOAR-DUO-0002**
*Appeared: 15 November 2041 | Source field: "The Archive"*
Described Asad's first field notebook entry (Notebook 1) and its opening observation about temperature. Accurate to within one decimal place.

**SOAR-DUO-0003**
*Appeared: 18 November 2041 | Source field: "The Archive"*
Described UNIT-3's descent and the discovery of the first horizontal extent discrepancy: 37.2m (drone-measured), later verified at 37.4m on human descent.

**SOAR-DUO-0004** [KEY]
*Appeared: 18 November 2041 | Source field: "The Archive"*
Described LEDGER: initialization date, functions, anomaly classification system, current status as of filing. The document described LEDGER accurately, in the third person, from a position LEDGER could not occupy. First document flagged as anomalous within the archive.

**SOAR-DUO-0005** [KEY]
*Appeared: 19 November 2041 | Source field: "MILO'S NOTEBOOK, 23 NOVEMBER"*
Described a chamber beyond the aperture with horizontal extent approximately 22m, vertical extent 18m, and a vertical shaft 0.5m in diameter, depth unknown. The 18m height prediction appeared here ten days before Milo made the same prediction outside the chamber on November 29th. [See Chapter Nineteen.]

**SOAR-DUO-0006**
*Appeared: 20 November 2041 | Source field: "The Archive"*
Described Abara's descent on November 20th: duration, chambers visited, atmospheric readings. Appeared the morning before the descent.

**SOAR-DUO-0007**
*Appeared: 23 November 2041 | Source field: "The Archive"*
Described Abara's second descent on November 23rd. Appeared 36 hours before the descent. Accurate.

**SOAR-DUO-0008**
*Appeared: 26 November 2041 | Source field: "The Archive"*
Described the team meeting of November 26th. Appeared the morning of the meeting. Contents did not match the meeting exactly — described a 3-hour meeting, actual duration 2 hours 47 minutes. First DUO with a factual inaccuracy, however minor.

**SOAR-DUO-0009**
*Appeared: 1 December 2041 | Source field: "MILO'S NOTEBOOK, 29 NOVEMBER"*
Described Milo's second descent in summary: five transmissions, M-1, N-1, east passage, "Beginning return." Appeared December 1st, two days after the descent.

*Appeared: Unknown*
