**CHAMBER MAPPING — CHAMBER A-1 (CONFIRMATION)**

*Entry confirmed at 09:51:17.*

UNIT-9 conducted a full survey using UNIT-7's GPS coordinates as a reference. Chamber A-1 was found at the expected location.

*Dimensions:*
- North-south axis: 41.2m ± 0.2m (UNIT-7: 41.3m ± 0.2m — match within tolerance)
- East-west axis: 38.7m ± 0.2m (UNIT-7: 38.8m ± 0.2m — match within tolerance)
- Height: 22.1m ± 0.1m (UNIT-7: 22.1m ± 0.1m — match)

This confirms Chamber A-1 as measured by UNIT-7.

*Temperature:*
21.4°C.

UNIT-7 measured 14.2°C in Chamber A-1 on 14 November.
UNIT-9 measures 21.4°C in Chamber A-1 on 16 November.
Difference: +7.2°C in 48 hours.

UNIT-9 diagnostic note: This thermal change is outside the expected range for any geological heating mechanism UNIT-9 can characterize. Passive heat transfer from surrounding rock at this depth would require approximately 400 years to produce a 7°C temperature change in a chamber of this volume. No active heat source has been detected. Flagging for expert review.

UNIT-9 checked for heat source indicators: geothermal vents (none), chemical reactions (no signature), electrical or electromagnetic activity (none detected). UNIT-9 cannot explain the thermal change.

*Atmospheric composition:*
Nitrogen 78.0%, oxygen 21.1%, argon 0.9%. Humidity 29%.

UNIT-7 measured humidity 34% on 14 November.
UNIT-9 measures humidity 29% on 16 November.
Difference: –5 percentage points in 48 hours.

A sealed chamber with no air exchange should have stable humidity unless: (a) the temperature change is driving moisture to condense or redistribute, (b) there is active desiccation of an unknown type, or (c) the chamber is not sealed and is exchanging air with a drier environment. UNIT-9 has not detected any connection to the exterior.

*North passage:*
The north passage described by UNIT-8 in Chamber B-1 (0.9m wide, 2.1m high) was not found in Chamber A-1. Chamber A-1 has no north passage. The presence of a north passage in B-1 and its absence in A-1 is either evidence that A-1 and B-1 are different physical spaces, or evidence that the passage was present in A-1 at a different time and is not present now.

UNIT-9 flagging: UNIT-9 cannot determine which.
