**DR. YEMI ABARA FIELD NOTES | 2041-11-25**
*Handwritten. Transcribed by Dr. Abara on return.*

*[09:02] Beginning descent. Shaft nominal. I've descended in the harness twice before, practice runs in the upper passages. The first thing I notice is how quiet it is.*

*[09:34] Chamber C-1. I took my own measurements before consulting SOAR-0011. I wanted a baseline — a reading that was mine, without the Archive. C-1 confirmed to within 0.3m on all dimensions. The measurements agree. I'm not sure if that's reassuring.*

*[09:49] Chamber E-1. Mark on the northwest wall is present. Deliberate horizontal line, 0.3m, incised to approximately 4mm. Milo described it. I measured it. It matches. I didn't touch it.*

*[10:03] I'm in Passage B, heading toward F-1. This is where Milo's transmission cut out — no, this is where he was an hour before his transmission cut out. He was here for the first time on the 20th. He was here a second time on the 22nd. I'm here on the 25th. Three different people, same passage. Each of us added something to the Archive.*

*[10:11] F-1. 14.2m cube, as mapped. Temperature 13.6°C. The south passage is here — this is where Milo went. This is where I'm going.*

*[10:14] In the south passage. I'm measuring as I walk: 20.1m to G-1's entrance. UNIT-13 recorded 19.8m. My measurement differs by 0.3m. I'm chalking this up to the fiber-optic tether adding friction to my movement, affecting pace count. I'll note it.*

*[10:19] Chamber G-1. 21.7m × 28.2m × 17.9m. Temperature 12.0°C. No marks on the walls.*

*[10:22] His pack is here.*

*[10:23] It's against the east wall, 4m from the entrance to the east passage. His notebook is in the outer pocket. The pack is closed. Nothing is disturbed.*

*[10:24] He put it down carefully. He wasn't running.*

*[10:31] I've read the notebook. The first forty pages: his preparation notes, his first descent (E-1, the prediction, the mark on the wall), his field observations from November 19th and 20th. Careful. Precise. He drew cross-sections of the passages, estimated gradients, noted temperature at each checkpoint.*

*[10:31] Then the November 22nd entries. His second descent. F-1. The south passage. G-1. His description of G-1 matches my measurements within 0.5m.*

*[10:32] The last three pages of the notebook are the November 22nd entries written in G-1. He describes the east passage. He describes the chamber at its end, estimating dimensions from the entrance. Then there is a line: "I am going to walk into the east passage now. I am leaving this notebook here."*

*[10:32] That is the last entry dated November 22nd.*

*[10:33] There are three more pages of writing after it.*

*[10:38] I need to stop and record this carefully. The three additional pages are dated November 25th. Today. The handwriting is Milo's. The content is — I need to read it again before I describe it.*

*[10:41] The November 25th pages describe, in field-note format, a descent by a second person. The gender is not specified. The person reaches G-1, finds the notebook, reads it, looks at the east passage, takes measurements. The measurements recorded in the November 25th pages are within 0.4m of the measurements I've recorded in the last thirty minutes.*

*[10:43] He wrote my field notes. He wrote them on November 22nd, three days before I descended.*

*[10:44] The November 25th pages end before the east passage. The person in the notes has not gone in. The last line of November 25th reads: "She is standing at the east wall. She has not decided yet."*

*[10:44] I haven't decided yet.*

*[10:51] I'm going back.*

*[10:52] I'm not going into the east passage. Not because I'm afraid — because Milo wrote that I would stand here and not decide, and the notebook is correct about everything else. If I go in, I am following his script. I don't know who wrote the script or how. But I know the script exists and I know it's accurate. Going in would confirm it. I need to tell Asad first. I need to tell him about the notebook before anyone does anything else.*

*[10:53] I'm taking the notebook.*

*[11:14] Back in F-1. Beginning return.*

*[12:08] Surface. All nominal.*
