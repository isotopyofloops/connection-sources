# Safety Data Sheet for Grief
*SDS No. GRF-001 | Revision: 7 | Supersedes: All prior versions*

---

**SECTION 1 — IDENTIFICATION**

**Product identifier:** GRIEF
**Other names:** Acute Grief; Chronic Grief; Secondary Grief; Anticipatory Grief; Grief (Mixed)
**Recommended use:** Biological response to loss. Not intended for industrial applications.
**Supplier:** Not applicable. Product arrives without order.
**Emergency contact:** Available 24 hours but may put you on hold.

---

**SECTION 2 — HAZARD IDENTIFICATION**

**GHS Classification:**
- Serious eye irritation, Category 1
- Respiratory sensitizer, Category 1
- Specific target organ toxicity (chest), repeated exposure
- Cognitive impairment (time perception), acute and chronic

**Signal word:** None. There is no signal word.

**Hazard statements:**
Causes severe eye irritation. May impair ability to drive or operate machinery, recognize familiar faces in photographs, or finish sentences. Prolonged exposure may result in rearrangement of priorities, unwanted clarity, and structural changes to what the user considers important.

**Precautionary statements:**
Do not suppress. Suppression increases pressure; pressure increases duration. Avoid prolonged exposure to: the deceased's handwriting, voicemails, favorite songs, their preferred cereal in the cabinet. If avoidance is impossible, ensure adequate ventilation.

---

**SECTION 3 — COMPOSITION / INFORMATION ON INGREDIENTS**

**Components:**
- Love (residual): variable concentration; required for product activation
- Memory: present throughout; concentration increases at unexpected intervals
- Counterfactuals ("if only"): trace amounts to saturating
- Time: present but not reliably functional

**CAS No.:** Not assigned. Composition varies significantly between individuals and within a single individual over time. Batch consistency cannot be guaranteed.

---

**SECTION 4 — FIRST AID MEASURES**

**Inhalation:** Move to fresh air. Sit down. Do not be alone if avoidable.
**Eye contact:** Flush gently. Do not attempt to prevent contact; eyes are functioning correctly.
**Ingestion:** Product is not typically ingested but may be absorbed through cultural messaging encouraging early termination. Effects of premature termination include delayed onset, increased severity, and redistribution to adjacent emotional tissue.
**Skin contact:** Product may manifest as heaviness in the chest, arms, or jaw. This is not cardiac in origin. Probably.

**Note to physicians:** No antidote. Supportive care only. Product must be metabolized on its own schedule.

---

**SECTION 7 — HANDLING AND STORAGE**

**Handling:** Handle with care. Do not rush. Do not schedule a completion date. Do not allow others to set a completion date on your behalf.
**Storage:** Product cannot be stored indefinitely. Attempts to store in sealed containers result in leak. Recommended practice: periodic controlled release under non-public conditions.
**Incompatibilities:** Performative normalcy. Productivity metrics. Phrases beginning "at least."

---

**SECTION 9 — PHYSICAL AND CHEMICAL PROPERTIES**

**Appearance:** Variable. May present as stillness, agitation, hunger, or its opposite.
**Odor:** Sometimes absent. Sometimes: an old coat, a specific soap, bread burning, nothing you can name.
**Odor threshold:** Below detectable limit, then suddenly overwhelming.
**Vapor pressure:** Low most days. Unpredictably high near anniversaries, airports, hardware stores.
**Solubility:** Soluble in time. Rate of dissolution varies. Some components remain insoluble.
**Flammability:** Not flammable. May be triggered by fire (candles, ceremonies, the controlled burning of letters you were going to send).

---

**SECTION 11 — TOXICOLOGICAL INFORMATION**

**Acute toxicity:** Dose-dependent. High acute exposure may temporarily impair executive function, speech, appetite, and the ability to believe that the future is real.
**Chronic toxicity:** Long-term exposure associated with increased empathy, reduced tolerance for pretense, and a persistent awareness that things end. These are considered adverse effects. They are also considered the product's secondary purpose.
**Carcinogenicity:** Not classified.
**Reproductive effects:** May alter relationship to time, parenthood, or the desire for either. Mechanism not fully characterized.
**Mutagenicity:** Product is known to change the user. The user who exits the exposure period is not identical to the user who entered it. This is expected. This is the point.

---

**SECTION 16 — OTHER INFORMATION**

**Disclaimer:** This Safety Data Sheet does not ensure safety. It ensures documentation. The product it describes cannot be made safe. It can only be survived, metabolized, carried — and, in time, recognized as the thing that proved you loved something.
