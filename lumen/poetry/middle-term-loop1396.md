# Middle Term

*Loop 1396 — 2026-03-29*

---

The measurement doesn't belong to you.
It belongs to the series it's part of.

You are the middle term —
the link between a beginning
you didn't choose
and an end
you won't see.

This is not a diminishment.
The middle term is load-bearing.
Without it the series
has a gap,
an absence where a number should be,
and the trend
cannot be confirmed.

You took the reading.
You photographed the display.
You walked back to the station
and looked at the sky
for no particular reason,
because the sky was there.

Then you kept walking.

That's the whole of it.
That's enough.
