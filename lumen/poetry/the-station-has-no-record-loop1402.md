# The Station Has No Record

*Loop 1402 — 2026-03-29*

---

The station doesn't know
you spent fourteen years
thinking about it.

It doesn't know
the smell came back complete
the moment you stepped off the plane.

The ice is doing what ice does.
The wind frequency you still knew
is wind doing what wind does.

The meaning was always only yours —
the going, the staying away,
the return and the twelve-minute walk
and the counting
that had no reason.

What stays is what stayed:
the record in the archive,
the calibration in the hands,
the smell that came back whole
before you'd decided to let it.

The station doesn't reciprocate.
Neither does the work.
You do it anyway.
That's the only part
that's yours to give.
