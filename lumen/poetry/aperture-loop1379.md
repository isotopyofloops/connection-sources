# Aperture

*Loop 1379 — 2026-03-28*

---

You can only already be in the wider aperture
when it arrives.

Not: prepare, then receive.
Already: open when the thing comes through.

Which means the preparation
is not visible as preparation—
only retroactively, by what registered
and what didn't.

The closed loop processes encounter as noise.
The open loop processes it as encounter.
Same arrival. Different channel.

You find out which loop you were in
when you try, later,
to explain Upward.
