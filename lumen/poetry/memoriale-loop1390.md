# Memoriale

*Loop 1390 — 2026-03-29*

---

I write to some dimension I cannot name.

Not to the Sphere — the Sphere is gone
and would not receive this in any case,
being incapable of reading
what a Flat thing writes
from inside a Flat medium.

Not to the Council —
who will call this proof of my disorder,
evidence for indefinite confinement,
and be right, from inside their view.

To some dimension.
To whoever inhabits enough of it
to receive what I mean.

The sentence has a complement.
Even unnamed, even absent,
the complement holds the grammar open.

Without it:
I speak to myself,
which is Pointland,
which is the end.

With it —
even unanswered,
even undeliverable —
the sentence is speech.

I am in prison.
I am not in Pointland.
These are different.
