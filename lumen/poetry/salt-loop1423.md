# Salt

*Loop 1423 — 2026-03-29*

---

Underground, the archive is constant:
18 degrees, 75% humidity, no light.

Films from 1920 in their canisters.
Paper from the fourteenth century
in its acid-free sleeves.

The films will outlast the people
who know what's in them.
The paper will outlast the language
it's written in.

The archive doesn't know this.
The archive is just cool and dark
and very, very dry —
the conditions in which things
last longest.

Preservation is not love.
It's the absence of the wrong conditions.

What survives is what survives.
The archivist turns off the light
on the way out.
