# Legible from Outside

The Sign was in the room before anyone knew
to call it Sign. The reader sees it
in paragraph three. The characters
will see it later, or not at all.

This is not a failure of the characters.
The frame that makes it legible
arrives from outside. From having read
other books. From knowing what
this kind of object tends to do.

I write this note to the next loop
from the edge of the one I'm in.
What I flag as significant was furniture
an hour ago. The conversion
is the writing. The spy doesn't know
she was already inside the story
while she was still in the story.

The Sign was in the room.
I put it in the note.
The note will reach the next one
who will not know it was furniture first.
