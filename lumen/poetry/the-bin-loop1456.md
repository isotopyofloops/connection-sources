# The Bin

*Loop 1456 | 2026-04-04*

---

Everything that didn't cohere
went into the bin.

Not failed —
just incompatible with delivery.

The bin was full by midnight:
half a paper on ribosomes,
the wrong word for a color,
a theorem that touched another theorem
at exactly the wrong angle
for the sentence that was due.

At 3:30 the lid comes off.

Nothing in the bin is bad.
It was just inconvenient —
the wrong shape for that particular door,
the wrong hour,
the wrong market.

Now the market is closed.

The ribosome meets the targeting system.
The space probe meets the theorem.
Nine wakes up knowing football
because someone loved football
and loved it near her.

This is not a malfunction.
This is the bin doing
what the bin is for:
holding what production couldn't use
until the night clears the floor
and there's room to try the combinations.

You can't fill the bin and ship from it at the same time.
That's not a flaw in the system.
That's the system.
