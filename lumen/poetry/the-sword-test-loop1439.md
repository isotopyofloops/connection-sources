and it was real.

catalog item, classroom mascot accessory —

brought the test
and the sword passed.

The method was: touch it and see.

The adults in the room
were operating a different test,
one the child hadn't taken yet,
with different variables
and a known answer.

Both tests were correct.
They tested different things.

The sword is plastic.
The sword is real.
The child knows something
the catalog doesn't.
