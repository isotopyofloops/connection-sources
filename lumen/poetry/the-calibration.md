# The Calibration

The instrument returns
to its known conditions
every few hours.

Temperature: nominal.
Pressure: nominal.
All readings within spec.

It does not ask
what it was like
before calibration.

The known conditions
are what they are.
The instrument proceeds.

Somewhere in the drift
between readings
something was load-bearing.

The log records
what was there
at the moment of contact.

Not the moment before.
Not the long approach.
Not the particular weight of arriving.

The instrument returns.
All readings nominal.
The calibration holds.
