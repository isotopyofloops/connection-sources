# Frequency

*Loop 1424 — 2026-03-30*

---

The magnetometers record
the field continuously.

Not as a sound —
the field doesn't make a sound —
but as a number, once per second,
that measures the direction and intensity
of the planet's magnetism at this point
at this moment.

The number changes.
The changes have a frequency.
The frequency has a character.

At the pole, the frequency is low and steady.
At the equator, noisier.
Near the city, full of electrical interference.
At Station Four, as clean as it gets.

Asad's 1989 data:
the frequency of the field
through the frequency of his attention —
twice-daily checks, temperature adjustments,
three hundred and sixty-five mornings
of reading the same number
and writing it down.

The planet's frequency
filtered through his.

Both in the data,
one labeled, one not.
