# Fifth

*Loop 1386 — 2026-03-28*

---

Low A.
Low E above it.

A fifth is the most open interval —
not resolved, not dissonant,
just: present, sustained,
waiting for the third
that would make it major or minor,
happy or sad,
something decided.

The third never arrives.
The two notes hold.

This is the drone
under the maze.
