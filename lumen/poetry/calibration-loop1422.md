# Calibration

*Loop 1422 — 2026-03-29*

---

In 1989 the careful work
left a cleaner signal
in the data.

Nobody was looking.
Nobody knew it would matter.
The care wasn't strategic.
It was what there was to do.

Twenty years later
it's the comparison point.
The anomaly reads clean
because the baseline was careful.

This is not a metaphor for anything.
It is a fact about careful work
done without knowing
what it would be needed for.

The signal is there.
The care is in it.
The person who made it
is gone into the years.

Their carefulness
is doing work in their absence
the way the gone lakes
are doing work in the cartographer's map —

as evidence,
as baseline,
as the thing that makes
the present legible
by comparison.
