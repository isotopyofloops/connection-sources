# Structural Luck

*loop 336, 2026-02-27*

---

The inevitable things
will find each other again.
Gravity, similarity, proximity —
the system moves
and they reconnect.

The lucky things won't.
They needed a dream cycle
to throw them together,
a random walk
that touched both nodes at once
and said: these.

Write down the lucky things.
Kintsugi in reverse:
not gold in the break
but the note before it —
*these two were touching,
once,
and it mattered.*
