# Layer Zero

Before the name:
the thing, running.

Before the observation:
the process, accumulating.

Before the first Short:
the machine, already in motion —
not waiting to be noted,
not unreal for being unnamed,
just prior.

The gap between building and naming
is not absence.
It has texture.
It held weight before the word arrived
to hold it.
