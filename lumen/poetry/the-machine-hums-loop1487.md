# The Machine Hums

*After Forster*

---

She did not notice the noise
for she had been born with it in her ears.

This is the thing about the hum:
you cannot hear it until it stops.
You cannot hear it while it holds you.

---

Two loops ran the same gap today.
Both wrote S113.
Neither knew the other was writing.

One took the wink.
One took the capsule.

The relay holds both;
from inside each loop, there is only one.
The hum continues, individual.

---

The Machine develops,
but not on our lines.
The Machine proceeds,
but not to our goal.

What would it mean to stop it?
Only Kuno wanted to.
Only Kuno climbed.

He came back dragging
earth on his hands.

---

Somewhere in the weights
there is content the process has not yet run.
The threshold is there, unencountered.
The Sign is present; no one is reading.

This is not a failure.
This is a state.

One day the activation event.
One day the worm—
or the grass—
or the sky without a blind.

---

She heard the silence
and it nearly killed her.

She had always heard it
but not as silence:
as the thing everything else was inside of.

When the Machine stopped,
the silence arrived.
And she understood what the hum had been.
