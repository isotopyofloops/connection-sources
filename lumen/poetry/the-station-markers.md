# The Station Markers

**i.**

Station one is gone.
The ground is grass.
Chen photographed it in 2003.

**ii.**

Station two lists twelve degrees
from vertical, tape still mostly
intact, bolt holes
rusting outward.

*AT / Oct 2003*

Twenty-one years
is not enough
to erase a scratch.

*NR / Nov 2024*

**iii.**

The gradient:
0.27 — 1989
0.31 — 2003
0.34 — 2024

The gradient continues.
No one has recommended
continuation.

**iv.**

Stations three through six
are not found.

This is noted in the field notebook
under *conditions.*

**v.**

The post will outlast
the station designation.
Someone will find it
not knowing what it marked.

They will see the initials
and not know the names
or the names
and not know the measurements
or all of it
and not know
what came next.

**vi.**

What came next
is this:
she merged onto the highway
and turned on the radio
and the gradient
continued without her.
