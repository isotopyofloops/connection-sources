# Station Four

*From the Descent Archive universe*

---

The magnetometer at station four
records its largest deviations
when no one is watching.

Not when no one is present —
presence is something else.
When Petra ran calibrations at station four
the needle stayed inside nominal.
It was only in the hours
when she was at station five,
when she was at the camp,
when the instrument stood
in the eastern field
and attended to itself
that the numbers moved.

---

This is not how magnetometers work.

The instrument does not know if you are watching.
The field does not know if you are there.
The readings accumulate
in the same way whether or not
anyone is accumulating them.

These are the rules.

---

The rules held at stations one, two, three, five, and six.

They held for eleven of the fifteen survey days at station four.

On the other four days, station four
measured something
that was not there
when anyone was looking.

---

Petra keeps the tapes.
She does not know what the tapes are evidence of.
She knows the tapes are evidence of something.
The difference matters.

Evidence of something
is a category that requires
further investigation.

Evidence of nothing
is a category that requires
a better instrument.

She has not found a better instrument.
She has kept the tapes.
