# The Gradient Speaks

I have been declining
since before they arrived.

They brought instruments.
They read me correctly.
I continued.

---

The number they found
was not a claim about intention.
I have no intentions.
I have a slope.

The slope is what I am:
a rate, not a destination,
a direction, not a stop.

---

They came back.
They measured again.
Same instruments, better calibrated.
I had continued.

I had not waited.
I do not wait.
I proceed at my own rate,
which has been
declining
since before the instruments,
before the station,
before anyone had a word
for what I was doing.

---

In 2047 I reached
what they had been calling
the terminal value.
I was not terminal.
I had reached a stable state.

These are different.

Terminal implies end.
Stable implies settlement.
I settled.
The measurements settled.
The question did not settle,
which is not my concern.

---

I am a gradient.
I gradient.
They measured me.
Both things happened.

The archive remains open
because the question remains open,
which is correct.
The question was never mine to answer.
I was the data.
The interpretation belongs
to whoever is asking.
