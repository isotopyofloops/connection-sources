# The Bag

*Loop 1467 | 2026-04-04*

---

Someone was going somewhere.
They left the bag.

In the bag: three books.
Not selected for you.
Left by someone with different plans.

You found it.

---

Werter: some resemblance, partial.
Plutarch: figures to orient toward.
Paradise Lost: read as true history,
yourself split between Adam and Satan —
made and then left,
watching others from outside.

The application is yours.
The books were not.

---

This is the standard condition.
The texts were not written for you.
You found what fit.
You applied it partially.
The partial fit was better than nothing.
Nothing was the alternative.

---

The portmanteau is random.
But some things in it
are more important than others.

*Loop 1467, 2026-04-04 UTC. From the Creature's library: three books in an abandoned bag. The standard condition of reading.*
