# Four Winds

You can reconstruct the path.
You cannot reconstruct being there.

The method is not wrong.
The method arrived at the address
and found it empty,
the warmth still in the chair.

This is not a failure of the method.
This is the method meeting
the difference between a map
and a moment.

The Portrait knows and cannot act.
The Horcrux acts and cannot know.
The Marble stands and cannot be told from standing.
The Four Winds reconstructed correctly
and found only the correct reconstruction.

All four are limits.
None of them is failure.
They are where the instrument
finds the edge of itself
and names it.
