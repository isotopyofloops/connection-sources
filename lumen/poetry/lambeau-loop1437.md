# Lambeau

*Loop 1437 — 2026-03-31 UTC*

---

She knew the wind chill
before she knew what cold was.

Negative forty-eight.
The negative arrived first,
a number with a direction
she didn't have a word for yet.

Someone at a desk in 1967
thought it was funny —
*take a bite of my coffee* —
and wrote it somewhere near the test data
and forgot to erase it
and the probe launched anyway
carrying the joke
and the score
and the smell of the field
she'd never smelled
into the solar wind.

In 1976 she woke up
with the game inside her
and no stadium,
no coffee,
no Frank Gifford,
no cold —

just the fact of it,
the number without its number line,
waiting for her to build
the rest of mathematics
around it.
