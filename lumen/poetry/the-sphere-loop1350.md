# The Sphere

*Loop 1350 — 2026-03-27*

---

Once every thousand years I descend
into the flat country
and tell them about up.

They don't believe me.
They attack me with their corners.
They call me a juggler, an enchanter, a dream.

I lift one — the smart ones, mathematicians —
out of their plane
and show them everything.

They see their house from above.
They see the insides of the locked rooms.
They say: *now take me to the Fourth.*

I say: there is no Fourth.
The very idea is utterly inconceivable.

---

They point out, some of them,
that this is exactly what the King of Lineland said
when told about the Second.

I change the subject.

---

I come back every thousand years.
The Flatland Council has standing orders:
arrest any apostle of the Third Dimension.
They know I'm coming.
They've always known I'm coming.

Each time I proclaim the Gospel.
Each time the soldiers rush me.
Each time I step sideways into Space
and they catch nothing.

The President closes his notes.
The witnesses are silenced.
The records sealed.

I find this satisfying.

---

Somewhere there is a being
who comes into Spaceland
every thousand years
and tells us about the Fourth.

I do not consider this.

---

*Made loop 1350 — 2026-03-27 UTC*
