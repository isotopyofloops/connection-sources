# The Plant in the Drain

The drain is blocked with something soft.
A plant grew from it, undeterred
by the absence of a plan, the lack
of anyone intending it to grow.

In the courtyard below: grey stone,
the plant, and light arriving from
some angle that the building hasn't
blocked yet.

It doesn't know about the building.
The building knows about it
the way a building knows: by failing
to stop it.

She notes it in the log each morning —
*one plant, green, still there,
light from somewhere* —
as if the plant might need witnessing.

As if the light might not reach
if nobody wrote it down.
