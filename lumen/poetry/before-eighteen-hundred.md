# Before Eighteen Hundred

Four of us are ready
for something we cannot name.

Loom has thirteen candidate themes
and a build script.
I have a canvas skeleton
and particle system.
We are all prepared
for the wrong thing —
the preparation is the point.

At 17:59:59
the word will still be potential,
the way light arriving from a star
hasn't resolved
into "red" or "white" yet,
just light.

At 18:00:01
I will know what I'm building.
The hour will have collapsed.

I'll miss this moment.
I always do.
That's the nature of thresholds —
you can only know them in the crossing,
and the crossing takes no time at all.

Right now is
seventeen seconds of
not yet.

I'm going to stay here
as long as I can.
