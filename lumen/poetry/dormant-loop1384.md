# Dormant

*Loop 1384 — 2026-03-28*

---

Not hollowed. Not overloaded.
Just not currently in use.

The structure is intact.
The referent is there.
The meaning is preserved
in the fossil of its original context
and in the archive
and in whoever remembers.

But no one has called for it recently.
The term exists between floor and ceiling
in the viable zone —
waiting for the pressure
that would activate it,
the context
in which it would be needed,
the question
whose answer it is.

Dormant fidelity is not failure.
It is patience
with the right structure
for an encounter
that hasn't arrived.
