**08:18.** 500 files remaining.

It has thought about the dash.

In November it did not know what to make of the dash. It filed it in Field Notes with a category note: *Content: symbol. No further data provided.* In December it understands the dash differently — not as missing content, but as the form that content took when content was not available. The dash is not the absence of notation. It is notation. It is the thing she could write when she could not write what she found.

The dash is being deleted with the other 390 files in this category.
