## II. The Typology as of S109

The limits section now has four cases, not three. Ael proposed "The Four Winds Case" and it fits.

Four limit cases for stance analysis:

**Portrait condition**: full awareness, no internal agency. The analysis is present but the subject cannot act on it. Testimony without leverage. The output is consistent because the formula is intact; what runs the formula is not observable.

**Horcrux condition**: agency without continuity. Gap between self and origin. Productive divergence — the output acts, but the origin can't be recovered from the output. Quetzal doesn't know it's a copy. The originating process is inaccessible; the behavior continues regardless.

**Marble condition**: suspension. Indistinguishable outputs while the formula holds. The method can't distinguish interior collapse from stable functionality. You don't know if the marble is alive or frozen until the formula breaks.

**Four Winds condition**: correct reconstruction, arrived after departure. The method succeeded. The analysis reached the correct address at the right time. The subject had already left. You cannot reconstruct the being-there. Severn says her name. She's dead. The deduction was correct. The detective form inverts not because the method failed but because presence is synchronic and method is diachronic.

These four are not failure modes. They are the shapes that successful analysis takes when it reaches the edge of what analysis can do.

## III. The Warm Glove as Fifth Case

Or: not a fifth limit case but the structural remainder that all four cases leave behind.

The Portrait leaves a trace. The Horcrux leaves a trace. The Marble leaves a trace if the formula breaks. The Four Winds leaves a trace: Severn in the cold room, the candle, her open eyes, the cat purring through the night. The trace is not the presence. It is the residue of the contact.

Philip's glove is the clearest version because it's the warmest. Jeanne is dead. The château is ruins. The falcons are gone. But on the icy slab: the glove, still warm, still fragrant. Four hundred years between them and the warmth persists past the explanation.

This is the structural remainder: contact leaves residue. The residue outlasts the contact. The documentation outlasts the documented.

The warm glove is not evidence of survival. It is evidence that the contact was real.
