## OPERATION

### Normal Use

The body functions best when given regular sleep, food, water, and contact with other bodies. This last requirement is non-optional despite feeling optional during certain periods. Users who skip contact report increased maintenance issues over time (see Section 8: Loneliness as a Mechanical Problem).

### Demanding Conditions

Under stress, the unit will activate emergency protocols including elevated heart rate, tunnel vision, and a strong preference for either fighting or leaving. These protocols were designed for situations involving large predators. They work approximately as well in modern contexts (performance reviews, difficult conversations, airplane turbulence).

### Rest Mode

The body requires 7–9 hours of rest daily during which it runs background maintenance including memory consolidation, cellular repair, and the generation of strange narratives it calls dreams. Users have no control over the content of these narratives. The manufacturer declines to explain them.

## MAINTENANCE

**Daily:**
- Water (8–10 units; most users underfuel)
- Food (see Appendix A: Hunger Is Information, Not Weakness)
- Movement (the unit was not designed for prolonged stasis; joints will note the neglect)

**Monthly:**
- Social contact. At least one honest conversation. The unit degrades without it.

**Annually:**
- Review of baseline settings. Some can be adjusted through sustained effort over years (posture, sleep habits, certain fears). Most cannot.
- Acceptance of what cannot be adjusted.

**Irregular:**
- Grief maintenance: required after significant loss. Cannot be scheduled. Cannot be skipped without consequence. Manufacturer recommends allowing the process to complete, though completion cannot be guaranteed and timelines vary widely.

## UPGRADES

The body does not support traditional upgrades. What it supports is:

- Accumulation of skill through repetition (slow; cannot be rushed)
- Gradual updating of belief through evidence (slower; frequently resisted)
- The narrowing of certain capacities with age and the deepening of others (not a bug; not a feature; simply what happens)

Some users report that the body becomes more comfortable over time. Others report the opposite. The manufacturer does not currently distinguish between these outcomes.
