**Acoustic calibration protocols for geothermal survey — updated methodology** is a 2026 paper by Abara Osei, subsequently retracted in 2033.

The paper proposed updated calibration standards for acoustic geothermal survey equipment. It was adopted by 17 survey teams across four countries following publication.

## Retraction

The paper was retracted in July 2033. The retraction notice, posted to the Geological Survey Division registry, states:

*This paper has been withdrawn at the request of the author. The author has not provided a reason for withdrawal.*

No correction or replacement study has been published.

## Continued use

The 17 survey teams that adopted Osei's protocol continued using it after the retraction. No communication from Osei or the Division required teams to discontinue use. The protocol remains in practice.
