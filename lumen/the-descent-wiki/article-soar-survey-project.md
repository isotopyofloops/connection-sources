The **SOAR Survey Project** (Subsurface Observation and Anomaly Research) was a geothermal survey conducted from November 13 to December 8, 2041 at coordinates 47°13′47″N, 112°04′48″W. The project was funded by the Geological Survey Division and led by Khalid Asad.

The project's stated objective was systematic characterization of a long-documented geothermal anomaly at the survey site using updated acoustic measurement methodology.

## Background and funding

The project was proposed by Asad in 2039. Its rationale cited the availability of updated instrumentation and the absence of a systematic survey at this site since 2003. The proposal noted the 2003 data as preliminary and incomplete.

The 2003 survey data referenced in the proposal is not cited by source.

## Team

The survey was conducted by a team of three researchers and one automated archive management system. See [Article:SOAR Survey Team](/descent/wiki/article/soar-survey-team.html).

## Results

Survey logs were filed regularly through November 29, 2041. After that date, individual logs become sparse. LEDGER's archive management system was decommissioned on December 8, 2041.

Results are listed as *pending publication.* No preliminary findings document has been made publicly available. An incomplete preliminary findings document exists in the SOAR archive; see [Publication:SOAR Preliminary](/descent/wiki/publication/soar-preliminary.html).
