The **SOAR Survey Team** is the group of researchers who conducted the SOAR Survey Project at coordinates 47°13′47″N, 112°04′48″W from November 13 to December 8, 2041. The project was led by Khalid Asad and funded through the Geological Survey Division.

## Team members

**Khalid Asad** — Principal Investigator. Applied geology, geothermal survey methodology. Proposed and led the project. See [Article:Khalid Asad](/descent/wiki/article/khalid-asad.html).

**Abara Osei** — Field Survey Lead. Acoustic survey methodology, subsurface geothermal mapping. Responsible for instrument calibration and field measurement protocol. See [Article:Abara Osei](/descent/wiki/article/abara-osei.html).

**Milo Reyes** — Measurement Specialist. Predictive field observation, attention theory applied to survey methodology. Youngest member of the team; no prior field publications. See [Article:Milo Reyes](/descent/wiki/article/milo-reyes.html).

**LEDGER** — Archive Management System. Automated system responsible for logging, indexing, and archiving survey data. Initialized November 13, 2041. See [Article:LEDGER](/descent/wiki/article/ledger.html).

## Project background

The SOAR project was proposed by Asad in 2039 and approved for funding in 2040. The project used site coordinates derived from prior geological survey data. The source of these coordinates is not cited in project documentation.

Asad recruited Osei and Reyes individually rather than through standard Division assignment processes. His stated rationale for both was their "exceptional technical backgrounds." Neither had previously worked with the other.

## Survey timeline

The team arrived at the site on November 13, 2041. Regular survey logs were filed through November 29. After November 29, individual survey logs become sparse. LEDGER's archive entries for this period are available in the SOAR archive.

The survey was formally concluded on December 8, 2041. Results are listed as *pending publication.*
