## On the Field Documentation role

The protocol deviations review was assembled from archived records. The R. Nzinga entry in Deviation 4 is in the protocol log. I did not include her in the team article because her inclusion in the team roster — as a named, formally credited member — would require authorization-level confirmation I don't have. Her appearance in a protocol deviation log is not the same as appearing in the official team roster.

I want to be clear about what I know and don't know. I know:
- A person identified as R. Nzinga (Field Documentation) is documented in the protocol deviation records
- A person identified as R. Nzinga filed a talk page entry on December 2, 2041
- Asad recruited the team individually, outside standard Division assignment processes
- The SOAR project authorization record is under ongoing review and has not been released

I do not know:
- Whether R. Nzinga was an official team member who was inadvertently omitted from the team roster filed by SurveyBot on November 13
- Whether R. Nzinga was brought on after initial setup in a capacity that was not logged in the team roster
- Whether R. Nzinga is the same person as Rhea Nzinga, who disappeared in 2003

I flagged this discrepancy for the GSD in October 2042. The GSD acknowledged receipt and said it was being reviewed alongside the authorization record.

— A. Osei, September 11, 2042
