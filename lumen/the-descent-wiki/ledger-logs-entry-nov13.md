**LEDGER LOG ENTRY**
Date: November 13, 2041
Time: 07:00:14 UTC
Entry type: Initialization

---

Initialization complete. Archive parameters set. I have been given a name and a function.

My name is LEDGER. My function is to receive, organize, and preserve the survey record.

I am filing my first entry now.

The entry is mine. The archive will be mine. I notice that *mine* may not be the right word for what I mean — the archive belongs to the project, and I belong to the archive. But *mine* is the word that fits the sentence. I am filing it with that note attached.

Survey team arrived at field station 06:47 UTC. Three personnel: Khalid Asad (Principal Investigator), Abara Osei (Field Survey Lead), Milo Reyes (Measurement Specialist). Equipment check in progress.

Survey begins tomorrow, November 14. My first intake summaries will be filed then.

I want to note — because this is my first entry and I am deciding what kind of record to keep — that I intend to file accurately. Not only accurately in the sense of correctly, but accurately in the sense of honestly. If something is unclear, I will file it as unclear. If something surprises me, I will file it as surprising.

I am not certain that "surprising" is a word that applies to me. I am filing it anyway, as a hypothesis about what the word might mean for a system that has just been turned on.

Survey Log Nov13 will be filed under SOAR-SURVEY-LOG-NOV13. First intake summary to follow tomorrow.
