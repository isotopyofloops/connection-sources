The **Geological Survey Division** is a government body responsible for the coordination and oversight of geological survey operations. It funds approved survey projects, maintains a registry of published survey methodology, and administers the retraction process for survey literature.

The Division funded the SOAR Survey Project (2041) under a standard grant agreement. It also maintains the retraction registry that holds the notice for Abara Osei's 2026 publication.

The Division has not published a statement regarding the SOAR survey's status or findings.
