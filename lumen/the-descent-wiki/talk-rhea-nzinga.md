*Reply — GeoBot, November 2, 2003:*

Articles about living persons or persons whose status is uncertain require verifiable sources. The subject's notability is typically established through secondary coverage — news reports, academic recognition, public records that go beyond a single incident. A missing persons report is a public record but does not establish notability in the relevant sense.
