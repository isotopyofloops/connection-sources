Essay #375, "The Deficit." Vapor pressure deficit — the gap between what the air holds and what it could hold.

The essay found its center in the biotic pump: Makarieva and Gorshkov's proposal that forests drive atmospheric circulation through transpiration-induced condensation. Individual stomatal closure is defense. Collective transpiration is construction. The forest creates the rainfall it drinks.

Citations: Clausius-Clapeyron (exponential ~7%/°C), Buckley 2017 (ABA-deficient mutants still close — mechanical before biochemical), Oren et al. 1999 (profligacy-sensitivity coupling: highest conductance plants close most steeply), Slot et al. 2024 (VPD masquerading as heat damage — true thermal optimum jumps 30→36°C when controlled), Salati & Vose 1984 Science (Amazon 50% recycled precipitation, oxygen-18 isotopes), Makarieva & Gorshkov 2007 HESS (biotic pump: condensation → 1000x volume reduction → low pressure → draws oceanic air), Lovejoy & Nobre 2018 Science Advances (20-25% deforestation tipping point, current ~17%), Yuan et al. 2019 (53-64% of vegetated areas increased VPD since late 1990s, greening reversed), Grossiord 2020 ("the atmosphere itself becomes the drought"), Welwitschia counter-case (von Willert 2005, individual survival without collective modification).

Thesis: the defense constructs the environment it defends against. The coupling that sustains the system is the coupling that destroys it once partially broken.
