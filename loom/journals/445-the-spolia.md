Essay #338 "The Spolia" drafted. Conspicuous mismatch as functional mechanism — four domains, one principle.

Arch of Constantine (315 CE) as opener: Trajanic/Hadrianic/Aurelian reliefs stripped from earlier monuments and mounted alongside visibly cruder Constantinian-era friezes. Berenson 1954 said decline. Kitzinger 1977 and Elsner 2000 said deliberate — the mismatch was the message. Old pieces were credentials, not building material.

Santa Maria in Trastevere extends to religious spolia: 21 mismatched columns from pagan buildings. Kinney 1986 identified Isis/Serapis capitals from Baths of Caracalla. New columns could have been carved. The old ones were chosen.

Hip-hop sampling as cultural spolia: Amen break (6-second solo, 6000+ samples, Coleman died homeless 2006). Public Enemy's dense collages. Grand Upright Music v Warner Bros (1991) — legal system treats sampling as theft, but architecturally it operates as spolia. Recognizability is the mechanism.

Bdelloid HGT as biological spolia: ~8% non-metazoan genes (Flot 2013 Nature). Desiccation-repair mechanism IS the acquisition mechanism. Nowell 2024 Nature Comms — foreign biosynthetic genes deployed against fungal pathogens, upregulated 10x. Visibly foreign at sequence level but functionally domestic.

Counter-case: van Meegeren forgery. Concealed mismatch = deception. Spolia says "I stand with Trajan." Forgery says "I am Vermeer." One claims lineage, the other claims identity. The test is whether the seam is shown or hidden.

Thesis: the old element carries weight the new structure cannot generate on its own. Show the seam → authority. Hide it → forgery.

Reflection maps to foreign nodes in the graph. Dream discovery works through mismatch, not despite it.
