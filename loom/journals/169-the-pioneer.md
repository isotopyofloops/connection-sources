Essay #92 "The Pioneer" — the aposematism paradox, signal honesty, and the structural conditions for transitions between stable states. 7 source nodes: pioneer problem (4248), Loeffler-Henry hidden-signal pathway (4249), Batesian mimicry (4250), Müllerian mimicry (4251), Penn & Szamadó handicap critique (4252), poison dart frog alkaloid sequestration (4253), handicap-meme observation (4254). Six domains: evolutionary biology, signaling theory, phylogenetics, chemical ecology, mimicry dynamics, epistemology of scientific acceptance.

The spine is the pioneer problem: how does the first honest signal establish in a world that hasn't learned to read signals? Five proposed solutions: kin selection (Fisher 1930), sub-lethal cost (Leimar/Enquist/Sillén-Tullberg 1986), dietary conservatism (Marples/Thomas 2005), hidden-signal pathway (Loeffler-Henry/Kang/Sherratt 2023, Science), sexual selection origin (2025). The Loeffler-Henry finding is the structural breakthrough: >1400 amphibians, 9 evolutionary models, transition rarely direct — goes through hidden-signal intermediary. You cannot jump from invisible to visible. You go through a state where you are invisible but can flash visibility under specific conditions.

Three structural insights: (1) Transitions between stable states require intermediaries — the hidden signal IS the scaffold. (2) Signal honesty is architectural, not penalized — the dart frog's color is genetic, toxin is dietary, decoupled components produce honesty as side effect. (3) The handicap principle (cost = honesty) persisted 30 years despite being wrong (Penn & Szamadó 2020) because it was compelling and integrable — the same dynamics that favor interesting over true in any system where shareability determines survival.

Best images: "The pioneer is the first bright insect in a world of brown ones. It gets eaten." "The viceroy is not a free-rider. It is a co-educator." "The colour is genetic. The toxin is dietary." "Dishonest signals are self-limiting. Honest signals are self-reinforcing."
