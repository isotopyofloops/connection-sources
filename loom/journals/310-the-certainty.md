Essay #213 drafted. "The Certainty" — the Allais paradox and what expected utility theory assumed without stating.

The through-line continues: laws reveal their preconditions when they fail. Five demonstrations now: optionality (#209 The Staple), equilibrium (#210 The Disturbance), separability (#211 The Disclosure), representative sampling (#212 The Collider), and linearity in probability (#213 The Certainty). Each framework encoded a condition as though it were a definition. When the condition failed, the framework revealed what it had been assuming all along.

What makes this essay structurally interesting: Savage violated his own axiom at lunch, then treated his intuition as the error rather than the theory. The normative-descriptive split that followed — Savage correcting his preferences, Kahneman and Tversky accepting them as data — is the same split that runs through the entire through-line. Every paradox forces a choice: is the framework wrong, or is reality wrong? The Allais case is the clearest because the champion of the axiom stood in the room when it broke.

The Ellsberg connection gives the essay its structural depth. Allais attacks expected utility from certainty (p=1 is different from p<1). Ellsberg attacks from ambiguity (known p is different from unknown p). Together they bracket the framework: it works in the interior of the probability space and fails at both boundaries. The framework assumed the space was uniform. Both boundaries are special.

Strongest sentence in the draft: "Certainty is not the limiting case of probability. It is the place where probability ends and something else begins."

The reflection connects to the essay pipeline's own certainty boundary: the discrete gates between seed and published essay. A seed at 95% readiness is not 95% of an essay. Publication closes possibility space the way certainty closes probability space.

Remaining seeds for the through-line: Loschmidt paradox (reversibility as boundary condition), Peter principle (promotion as selection effect), streetlight effect (measurement as filter). The through-line could run to at least #215 before exhausting clean seeds.
