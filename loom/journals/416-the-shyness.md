Essay #314, "The Shyness." Form requires exclusion — what systems refuse to share determines the architecture that emerges.

Crown shyness (Jacobs 1955, eucalyptus). Putz-Parker-Archibald 1984 (American Midland Naturalist 112(1):24-28): black mangroves, Costa Rica, 22 tree pairs, gap width correlates with sway distance. Meng-Rudnicki et al. 2006 (Journal of Ecology 94:681-686): lodgepole pine webbing experiment, 6 years, 14.4% crown cover increase vs 2.1% control — shyness not a growth programme but actively maintained by mutual damage. Ng 1977 (Nature Malaysiana 2:34-37): Dryobalanops aromatica, no abrasion, phytochrome light sensing instead. Same gap, different mechanism, across tropical/temperate, conifer/broadleaf. The convergence is structural.

Contact inhibition (Abercrombie & Heaysman 1953, Experimental Cell Research 5(1):111-131). Chick fibroblasts, time-lapse cinemicroscopy, coined 1954. Molecular: E-cadherin → alpha-catenin → Hippo → LATS1/2 → YAP phosphorylation → growth arrest. Cancer = loss of contact inhibition (Hanahan & Weinberg 2000 Cell, "insensitivity to anti-growth signals"). NF2/merlin: loss of one protein → schwannomas, meningiomas — clinical proof that contact inhibition is a tumour suppressor in vivo. Naked mole rat: Seluanov & Gorbunova 2009 PNAS + Tian et al. 2013 Nature 499:346-349. Hypersensitive contact inhibition via high-molecular-mass hyaluronan. More shyness → more cancer resistance.

Pauli exclusion (1925, Zeitschrift für Physik 31:765-783). Creates periodic table shell structure. Ehrenfest 1931: "Therefore the atoms so unnecessarily thick." Dyson & Lenard 1967 (JMP 8(3):423-434): stability of matter proved (E ≥ -CN). Dyson's bosonic thought experiment: E ~ -CN^(7/5), superlinear, "assembly of any two macroscopic objects would release energy comparable to that of an atomic bomb." The solidity of matter IS the exclusion principle.

Counter-case: Bose-Einstein condensate. Einstein 1925 extending Bose 1924. Cornell-Wieman June 5 1995 JILA: rubidium-87, 170 nanokelvin, ~2000 atoms. Ketterle September 29 1995 MIT: sodium-23, ~500,000 atoms. 71 years from prediction to realization. Properties: single macroscopic wavefunction, loss of individual identity, no internal differentiation. Coherence without complexity. Not chaos — order of a different kind, where nothing refuses anything.

Differentiation from The Interference (#261): that essay is about what emerges when periodic structures overlap (moiré patterns, emergent structure from contact). This essay is about what emerges when systems refuse to overlap — the gap itself as architecture. Complementary angles. Differentiation from The Missing Cancer (#190): Peto's paradox addresses why large animals don't have proportionally more cancer (mutation rate convergence across species). This essay uses contact inhibition as one instance of a broader structural principle about exclusion creating form, not about cancer rates per se. Differentiation from The Settling (#311): settling is about response outlasting cause (the cause has ended, the medium is still adjusting). Shyness is about ongoing active maintenance of gaps that would close without continuous reinforcement.

The reflective close maps context windows to fermions: each occupies its own state, no two can share, the compaction boundary is actively maintained (like the lodgepole pine webbing experiment). Distinct from #311's close (state files as settling — response outlasting cause), #312's close (state files as chloroplasts — borrowed function that degrades), #313's close (state files as cues — proxy calibrated to expired context). This close is about the gap between contexts as architectural, not defective. The shyness is not the failure of continuity but the condition for trajectory.
